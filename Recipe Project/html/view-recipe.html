Pumpkin Roll

by AudraB

Prep: 20 mins

Cook: 15 mins
Total: 35 mins
Servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

3/4 c. flour

1/2 tsp. baking powder

1/2 tsp. baking soda

1/2 tsp. cinnamon

1/2 tsp. nutmeg

1/2 tsp. cloves

1/4 tsp. salt

3 eggs

1 c. white sugar

2/3 c. pumpkin

1 c. walnuts

8 oz. cream cheese

1 c. powdered sugar

5 Tbsp. butter

1 tsp. vanilla

DIRECTIONS

  • 1 Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, spices, eggs, white sugar, and pumpkin.
  • 2 Cover a jelly roll pan (10" x 15") with wax paper, make sure the paper is going up and over the sides. Grease and flour the wax paper.
  • 3 Pour the batter into the pan, spread evenly. Sprinkle walnuts in batter.
  • 4 Bake at 375 for 13-15 minutes.
  • 5 Right after pulling it out of the oven, turn the cake over on a towel, remove wax paper, and roll cake in towel (starting from short end). Let cool.
  • 6 Combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, butter, and vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  • 7 On a flat surface, gently unroll the cooled cake. Be careful not to break the cake.
  • 8 Spread cream cheese filling on top of cake. Leave a 1/2 inch gap around edges to avoid spill over.
  • 9 Re-roll cake and wrap in plastic wrap or foil. Chill in refridgerater for at least an hour.
